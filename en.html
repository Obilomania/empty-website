Our Activity

Romgaz is the largest natural gas producer and the main supplier in Romania. The company is listed on Bucharest Stock Exchange and GDRs are transacted on London Stock Exchange. Geological exploration, natural gas production, natural gas storage and electric power production represent only a part of Romgaz activity.

House of Natural Gas

Romgaz respects the tradition and promotes the gas industry, encouraging the development of a positive awareness at its level, by establishing the House Of Natural Gas.

Despre noi
