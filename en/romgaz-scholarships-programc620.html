"ROMGAZ Scholarships" Program

„ROMGAZ Scholarships”

"ROMGAZ Scholarships" aims at ambitious students with outstanding academic results, young people with well-established career goals, to whom we offer financial support during their university studies, and at the same time the opportunity to become ROMGAZ team professionals.

For this project, S.N.G.N. Romgaz S.A. concluded collaboration agreements with prestigious Universities in our country that specialize in the field of natural gas exploration and exploitation.

We believe that any challenges, but also opportunities and possibilities, will lay the foundations for professional relationships based on mutual respect, and will contribute both to improvement and strengthening of ROMGAZ team.

During the summer of 2021, ROMGAZ started to develop partnerships with Romanian universities specializing in energy. Within this project we identified students with outstanding academic results, young people with well-established career goals, who received financial support during their university studies. The aim of these scholarships was to prepare professionals in order to hire them within S.N.G.N. Romgaz S.A.

More than a monthly scholarship

  • Scholarship in amount of RON 1500 per month until completion of studies;
  • Internships  guided by tutors;
  • Assistance in preparing thesis/dissertation;
  • Professional and educational development opportunities in the field of energy;
  • Possibility to be hired by the company.

ROMGAZ granted scholarships to 3rd and 4th year students and to master’s students studying the following specializations: 

  • Hydrocarbon Transportation, Storage and Distribution
  • Natural Gas Engineering and Management
  • Geological Engineering
  • Applied Geology
  • Applied Geophysics
  • Management of Energy
  • Oil and Gas Engineering 

The beneficiaries of these scholarships were the undergraduate students and master’s students who became aware of the fact that getting these scholarships is a natural consequence of their education and involvement in the studies as well as a motivation to further improve their educational performance. 

During this scholarship program, 16 undergraduate and master’s students benefited from ROMGAZ scholarships and 13 of them became our colleagues.
