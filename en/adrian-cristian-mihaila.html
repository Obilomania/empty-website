Adrian Cristian MIHĂILĂ

„I graduated the University of Bucharest, Faculty of Geology and Geophysics, majoring in Geological Resources Engineering.

This was an important choice in my life because I have wanted to become a geologist ever since I was a kid and I first discovered nature science. ROMGAZ scholarship was the perfect opportunity for me to start a professional activity that I very much desired. My ROMGAZ mentor is Ioan MOGA, head of the Resources and Reserves Evaluation Office, where I am hired now, and he provided me understanding and advice every time I needed it. Besides him, I found support and help in my colleagues every time I needed it. I would recommend ROMGAZ scholarship to any student that wishes to start a career in a place where appreciation and understanding are found.”

Despre noi

Romgaz undertakes geological exploration in order to discover new gas reserves, produces methane by exploiting the reservoirs included in the company portfolio, stores natural gas in the underground deposits, interventions, workover and special operations on wells and technological transport.
