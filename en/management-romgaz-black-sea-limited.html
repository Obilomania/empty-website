Management Romgaz Black Sea Limited

Executive Management Romgaz Black Sea Limited

  • As of July 04, 2023

    Diana – Andreea Lupu became Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of ROMGAZ BLACK SEA LIMITED in July 2023. 
    From the current position, Mrs. Lupu coordinates the activities performed within ROMGAZ BLACK SEA LIMITED related to NEPTUN DEEP project. 
    Mrs. Lupu held previously, various positions within S.N.G.N. Romgaz S.A., the largest natural gas producer in Romania, being responsible, among others for coordinating S.N.G.N. Romgaz S.A. transition in the transaction and the transfer of all shares issued by (representing 100% of the share capital) ExxonMobil Exploration and Production Romania Limited which holds 50% of the rights and obligations under the Petroleum Agreement for the deep water area of XIX Neptun Block in the Black Sea (”NEPTUN DEEP”).
    She graduated the Engineering Faculty of Lucian Blaga Sibiu University, major in Hydrocarbon Transmission, Distribution and Storage, a Master’s Degree in Natural Gas Engineering and Management and a PhD in the field of Mine, Petroleum and Gas within the same University of Petroleum and Gas, Ploiesti.

Despre noi

Romgaz undertakes geological exploration in order to discover new gas reserves, produces methane by exploiting the reservoirs included in the company portfolio, stores natural gas in the underground deposits, interventions, workover and special operations on wells and technological transport.
